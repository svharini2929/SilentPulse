## 📖 Project Overview & Deep Dive

Medical technology has made quantum leaps in recent years, but legacy systems leave behind a massive data gap. In thousands of clinics, diagnostic archives, and underserved emergency rooms worldwide, vital cardiovascular histories remain locked on physical, fading sheets of pink grid paper. 

When a patient experiences an acute cardiac event, manually transcribing historical paper reports or waiting for modern hardware integrations wastes crucial clinical minutes. **SilentPulse** solves this problem by using automated computer vision and deep learning to instantly convert static, analogue paper ECG sheets into actionable, digital time-series arrays—unlocking predictive clinical intelligence from physical waste.
